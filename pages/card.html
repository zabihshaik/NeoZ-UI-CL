Card

Cards are flexible and extensible content containers. They include options for headers and footers, images, a wide variety of content, different background colors, and powerful display options. They can be used to give any information for the user, for eg. in Products page of an ecommerce website.

Vertical Card

Vertical Cards are Basic Cards which may contain product image, product name, product details, some buttons and icons. You can use card-container for card layout, and for other components, use card-image, card-author, card-content , etc. as shown below.
